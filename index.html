About Me

My first computer was a 300MHz IBM with a monitor that sat atop. It came with Windows 98 and new on the market 56k. The world was at my fingertips. I could learn whatever I wanted and I didn't need to leave the house. I soon upgraded it to Windows 2000, updated the graphics cards, sound cards, and even added CD-RW. I taught myself to program with Visual Basic, I made websites with Frontpage, and by the time Windows XP came out I was hacking other computers.

I have over 20 years of experience in web design and just as long in hardware/software support. I am the CEO and Lead Web Designer of dearborn.dev, an IT and Web Development company.

Current Focus: These days I'm deep into AI tooling, workflow automation with n8n, and building software that makes life easier for small businesses and local communities.

Outside of Tech: When I'm not in front of a screen, I'm busy being a dad and getting into birdwatching. Yes, birdwatching. Don't knock it till you try it.

I decided to make this page as a "developer profile" and went with the iconic MySpace theme of the early 2000s, music player included. Deciding on a song is still one of the most difficult decisions for this page.
